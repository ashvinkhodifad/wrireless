What you can do with this integration

You can send new shipments to Shipstation and update them, create warehouses, mark order as shipped, add fund and many others options.

Shipstation configuration

During the installation of the module, you will have to provide Key, Secret and StoreId of your Shipstation account.

If you do not have an account, you can easily request a free 30 day trial. Sign up with Shipstation

You can find Key and Secret in Account Settings/Api Settings:


Now in Account Settings, go to Selling channels and copy the Id of the store.


That's it! Every time you have a Sales Order in "sale" status, it will be sent automatically to Shipstation.

This is the default mapping of Sales Orders to Shipstation Orders:

You can modify this behaviour and the fields sent to Shipstation. Check the mapping under Mappings tab in Cenit module and create your own mappings.
